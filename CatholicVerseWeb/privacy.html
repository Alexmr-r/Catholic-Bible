Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2025

CatholicVerse ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you use CatholicVerse:

  • Account information: name and email address when you register.
  • Usage data: app usage patterns, features accessed, and reading progress to improve the experience.
  • Purchase information: subscription status managed by Apple App Store and Google Play. We do not store your payment details.
  • Device information: device type and OS version for technical support purposes.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To provide and personalize your experience in the app.
  • To process subscription purchases via RevenueCat, Apple, and Google.
  • To send account-related communications (password reset, service updates).
  • To improve the app based on usage patterns.

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • RevenueCat — for subscription billing management.
  • Resend — for transactional email delivery.
  • Google / Apple — for optional social sign-in and in-app purchases.

Each of these services has its own privacy policy and data practices.

4. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You can request deletion of your account and personal data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures including encrypted data transmission (HTTPS) and hashed passwords. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

7. Children's Privacy

CatholicVerse is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes via the app or email.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
